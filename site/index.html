Features

Built for people who care
about their disk.

Not another bloated utility. DriveMosaic is fast, focused, and stays out of your way.

Treemap Visualization

Every file and folder rendered as proportional blocks. Big files jump out. No hunting through Finder.

Fast Scanning

Built on BSD's fts API — the same low-level interface professional disk utilities use. Hundreds of thousands of files per second.

Drill-Down Navigation

Click into any folder. Breadcrumb bar tracks your path. CMD+Click to reveal in Finder. Escape to go back.

Drag-to-Delete Pro

Drag space hogs to the collector. Review what you're removing. One click to trash. Auto-rescan confirms the cleanup.

No Sandbox Limits

Distributed outside the App Store so it can actually scan your full disk. Grant Full Disk Access and see everything.

Real-Time Progress

Live scan counter, elapsed time, and scan rate. You always know what's happening — no staring at a spinner.
