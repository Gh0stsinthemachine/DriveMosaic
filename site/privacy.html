Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

The short version: DriveMosaic does not collect, transmit, or sell your file data. Your disk contents never leave your Mac.

What DriveMosaic does

DriveMosaic reads your filesystem metadata (file names, sizes, directory structure) entirely on your device to generate the visual treemap. This analysis happens locally — nothing about your files, folders, or their contents is ever sent to any server.

What we collect

We collect only one piece of information: your email address, submitted when you first open the app. This is used to:

  • Send release notes and update announcements
  • Communicate important security or product changes

Your email is stored securely by Formspree (our email collection service) and is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

What we do not collect

  • File names, paths, or contents — your disk contents never leave your device
  • Usage analytics or telemetry — we don't track how you use the app
  • Crash reports — DriveMosaic has no crash reporting SDK
  • Location data — never collected
  • Device identifiers — beyond what Polar generates for license activation (see below)

License key activation

If you purchase DriveMosaic Pro, your license key is validated through Polar.sh. During activation, Polar receives your license key and generates an instance identifier to track your activation slot. No other personal data is transmitted. Polar's privacy policy is available at polar.sh/legal/privacy.

Third-party services

  • Formspree — Handles email collection. Privacy policy →
  • Polar.sh — Handles Pro license payments and activation. Privacy policy →
  • Cloudflare — Hosts this website. Standard web server logs (IP, user agent, timestamp) may be retained per Cloudflare's policy. Privacy policy →

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you are a DriveMosaic user. You can request deletion at any time by emailing support@drivemosaic.app and we will remove it within 7 days.

Children's privacy

DriveMosaic is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify users via email and update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@drivemosaic.app. We're a small team and we respond personally.
